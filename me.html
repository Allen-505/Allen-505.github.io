backstory of David's Exotics

This is How it started.

I have had a lifelong passion for pets, starting in my childhood when I enjoyed giving my friends guppies and goldfish as gifts. As I grew older and gained more knowledge about pets, people around me began to see me as an expert in the field. When friends and teachers started asking me where they could buy these pets, I turned my hobby into a business, selling fish as a source of passive income during my school years. However, I was encouraged to go to college, which was not in line with my passions. So, I made the decision to drop out of higher education and pursue my true passions. I struggled for a few years, working a 9 to 5 job and learning about the struggles and determination it takes to follow your passions. It was during this time that I also gained knowledge about exotic pets and unique animals that are not commonly found in households. This ultimately led me to start my own business, David's Exotics.
